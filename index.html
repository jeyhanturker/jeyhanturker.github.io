About Jeyhan

Jeyhan is an R&B/soul-influenced pop artist from the Washington D.C. area. His sound is a mixture of 90s R&B, current trap beats, & Turkish folk music. Some of his favorite artists that have influenced his sound are Lauryn Hill, Beyoncé, and Turkish singer Tarkan. To help him navigate through his mental health journey, Jeyhan has centered his new music around themes of self-love and inner healing, which he hopes can help listeners see their true potential and live life on their own terms.

Accomplishments & Notable Works

Since starting his recorded music career in 2015, Jeyhan has experienced great success in the Washington D.C. and Miami music scenes. He has won the Cherry Blossom's Got Talent competition (prize included performing on national television during the National Cherry Blossom Parade in D.C.), came second in the Apple Blossom's Got Talent competition, and won The O competition (prize included a complimentary 5-song EP, which he released in 2018). Additionally, Jeyhan's song "Games" was played on WPGC 95.5 FM, which is the definitive hit R&B station of the DMV. He was one of the only independent artists to have their song played on that staion. Jeyhan's notable music releases include:

  • Rearview (2021) - Over 15,000 streams
  • Make Me Feel (2020) - Over 100,000 streams
  • Tease (2019) - Over 160,000 streams
  • Altered Love [EP] (2018) - Over 50,000 streams

Live Shows

Live performance has always been a priority for Jeyhan and is an integral part of his artistry. He closely studies the precision of top-tier performers such as Michael Jackson, Beyonce, and Bruno Mars to take inspiration and model his style off of. He is a powerhouse vocalist & vibrant performer with a goal of bringing back the "triple-threat" superstar quality of artists popular in the 80s, 90s, and early 2000s. Jeyhan is known for his performance style described by fans as Beyoncé meets Bruno Mars meets Sam Smith. When going to see Jeyhan live, expect acrobatic vocals, detailed choreography, precise staging, and an inviting communal experience.
